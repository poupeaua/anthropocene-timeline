Add Content

Adding content to the timeline is easy and free. To ensure content quality here are the 4 steps a suggested new content goes through:

Steps

1

Create your content: Submit a pull request on Github. The new content should respect the template, must be in english and be sourced.

2

Content verification: Your content will be reviewed by the main contributors to make sure it makes sense here and is sourced.

3

Multilingual verification: As this website is multilingual, your content must be translated into all supported languages. You can suggest translations using your preferred service, and native-speaking referees will review them.

4

Validation: Your content is approved.

Your content is now live!
